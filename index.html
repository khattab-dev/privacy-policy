Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1/23/2026

This Privacy Policy explains how El Rewad (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information when you use our mobile application (the “App”).

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information (Employees Only)

We collect email addresses and passwords only for employees who log in to manage and update orders. This information is used solely for authentication and access control.

1.2 Order Tracking Information

Users can track orders using an Order ID without creating an account or logging in. We do not collect personal information from users when they track orders.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Financial or payment information
  • Credit or debit card data
  • Customer personal profiles
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or media
  • Sensitive personal information

3. How We Use Information

We use the collected information only to:

  • Authenticate employees
  • Allow employees to manage and update orders
  • Display order status based on Order ID
  • Maintain app security and stability

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties. Data may be disclosed only if required by law or legal process.

5. Data Storage & Security

We apply reasonable security measures to protect stored data, including:

  • Secure authentication mechanisms
  • Restricted access to employee accounts
  • Industry-standard security practices

6. Data Retention

  • Employee account data is retained while the account is active.
  • Order-related data is retained only as required for operational purposes.

Data is deleted or deactivated when no longer needed.

7. User Rights

Employees may request:

  • Access to their account data
  • Correction or deletion of their account

Users tracking orders by Order ID do not provide personal data.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App with an updated date.
